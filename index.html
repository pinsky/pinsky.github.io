A bit about Myself

Md. Armanul Haque is an Associate Professor in Information Science and Library Management at the University of Rajshahi, Bangladesh. Currently pursuing a PhD in Information Management at Wuhan University, China, his research interests include knowledge sharing, bibliometric analysis, social media, and reading habits. With a passion for advancing information science, he actively collaborates with peers globally and fosters a culture of reading among students.
